Raptor v2.0

This car featured an aerodynamic body, a lighter chassis with an aggressive stance to give sharper turning confidence at track and flexibility at roads. In terms of innovation, it had active sensors throughout the body and semi-to-total autonomous driveability. The aim was to build an old-fashioned car equipped with all the modern features of an electric vehicle - a track car disguised as a vintage classic.
