ELECTRONICS & COMPUTER ENGINEER

Today, taking a specific car in picture, one can say that 70% of the cost goes towards electronic components and the rest goes to non electronic parts such as body, powertrain etc. Electronics and Computer Engineers will be responsible for design and development of all the Low Voltage Components like PCB, Arduino, DAQ, and driver interface. It's a cross-functional and multidisciplinary department which focuses mainly on information & data tracking as well as simplifying the tasks. Apart from this, the team is constantly working on implementing autonomous driving into the vehicle.

What You'll Do

  • Electronics engineer will be responsible for designing and troubleshooting PCBs, Arduino, Raspberry Pi and HMI.
  • Computer engineer will be responsible for developing algorithms and implementing automation wherever necessary.
  • You will research on data acquisition and driver assistance systems, and work on its implementation
  • You have a strong passion to code and also have an innovative approach to solve problems. Analytical and reasoning ability is a must.
