ELECTRICAL ENGINEER

They are the nervous system of the entire electric vehicle anatomy. The major work lies on the powertrain and energy source. This includes lithium battery, motor controller and motor, solar panels, car lights, and waterproof connections. Apart from this, testing battery packs, working on the motor and designing driver circuits, developing and designing the wiring system, power electronics circuit design, and the list just goes on.

What You'll Do

  • You have the curiosity to understand and gain in-depth knowledge of all the components that goes into the vehicle.
  • You are a team player and can collaborate with mechanical teams and also other clubs.
  • You will be the go-to person and have the ability to solve problems in any condition.
