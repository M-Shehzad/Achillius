MECHANICAL ENGINEER

The Mechanical Engineering team designs and manufactures the entire mechanical system of the car including the suspension, steering, chassis, gearbox and braking systems. They account for the ballast and weight distribution when making all of their design decisions. They are also the "jack-of-all-trades," balancing all the needs of other subsystems. Electrical team needs a place to mount the battery? No problem - we got that covered.

What You'll Do

  • You will be responsible for designing and analysing various parts, assemblies and subsystems of the car, from scratch.
  • You are an independent learner and always seek guidance.
  • You have a strong passion to experiment, explore, iterate and work until the goal is met.
  • You have excellent communication skills and harness team culture.
