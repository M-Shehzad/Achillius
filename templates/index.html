About Us

We are Team Achillius - a student group under St Joseph Engineering College, Mangalore. Every year, we design and manufacture a solar powered electric vehicle that competes in prestigious national level competitions. Students from multiple departments come together and get an intense experience as they undergo real world engineering challenges everyday. Apart from technical aspects, members gain invaluable business and project management skills.

Team Achillius was started in 2017 as the first of its kind in Mangalore. Since then, we have successfully built TWO electric solar vehicles and won many accolades. Our main event is the Electric Solar Vehicle Championship which is organised by Imperial Society of Innovative Engineers and Automotive Research Association of India.
